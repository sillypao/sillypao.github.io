### Thanks for checking in, my name is Paola and I'm currently a cybersecurity student! 

I created Safe Internet 4 Me as a passion project to help others learn more about internet safety and cybersecurity. Finding relevant resources as a cybersecurity student has been tough, I made this website so that isn't the case for many. I hope this corner of the internet can help you in some sort of way. Whether it's learning about possible scholarships, ways to stay safe on the internet, or guidance towards a career in cybersecurity, Safe Internet 4 Me is here to help!

Throughout my cybersecurity journey, I have obtained several certifications all thanks to the resources that have made it possible. My current certifications include: 
- CompTIA A+
- CompTIA Security+
- CompTIA Network+
- ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity
